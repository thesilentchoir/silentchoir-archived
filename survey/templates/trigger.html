NOTICE OF POTENTIAL RISKS

As part of creating your report, you will be asked a series of questions related to incidents of violence or harassment.

Discussing harassment, abuse and violence is often a painful process that can trigger a number of intense emotional reactions. Please note that sharing your story is completely voluntary, and you should feel absolutely no obligation to share your story if you are not yet ready.

Are you in a safe place right now? Do you have a support system or other resources to access should you feel triggered? Before proceeding, please consider whether you are in the right place to be completing this report.

If at any point you feel you need emotional support of any kind, please call the National Sexual Assault Telephone Hotline (800.656.HOPE (4673)) where you will be able to speak with a trained professional.
